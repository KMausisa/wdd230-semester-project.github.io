Chamber Purpose and Goals

As small local business continues to face trials during the pandemic, the city is here to support each and every one of them. This website is to raise additional awareness by hosting events that will involve the community, which will increase the exposure of these businesses. Overall, bringing the community together through activities and volunteer opportunities will foster and safe loving bond between friends and family.
